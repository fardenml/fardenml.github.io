About

My name is Michael Farden. I am currently a senior computer engineering student at Clarkson University, minoring in software engineering and mathematics. From June to August 2017, I interned as a software engineer at Harris Corporation in Rochester, NY. I was a member of the Software Design Verification (SDV) department and wrote test cases for software defined, military radios using C# and .NET framework. I will be working full time as a software engineer for Harris after graduation.
